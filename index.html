We are UnivBid,
Your Campus Marketplace

Your university's trusted auction platform where you can list, bid, and manage auctions securely and in real-time. UnivBid is designed to help students create, manage, and participate in auctions within a university setting. Whether you're buying or selling, UnivBid provides an easy-to-use interface for all your auction needs.

Platform Features

  • List your items for auction with just a few clicks.
  • Place bids on items securely and in real-time.
  • Manage and monitor your auctions through a user-friendly dashboard.
  • All auctions are powered by Firebase for secure and real-time updates.
  • A clean, responsive interface built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
